Overview of major programming languages

Software development is a dynamic field. New and in-demand programming languages, frameworks and technologies can emerge, rise to fame, and then fade away in the course of a few years. Developers need to constantly be learning new skills to stay relevant.

Every year, Stack Overflow (one of the most popular websites featuring questions and answers on software development), conducts an Annual Developer Survey asking the developer community about everything from their favorite technologies to their job preferences. Part of the results, of course, shows how different programming languages rank in terms of popularity.
